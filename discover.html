A new Building is being Constructed on La Cejita

The building's amenities will enhance quality of life for La Cejita residents. There will be a large lobby area with seating, wifi, and charging stations open to the public.

The rooftop deck will offer scenic views of the town and a place for residents to gather for events.

There are also plans for a fitness center for building tenants and possibly a daycare facility.
